We provide online therapy sessions, mindfulness workshops, support groups, and educational resources for mental health awareness and coping strategies.

Most of our group sessions and educational resources are free. Individual therapy sessions may have associated costs, but we offer sliding scale fees based on income.

For immediate crisis support, please call the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline at 1-800-273-8255 or text HOME to 444444 for crisis text line support.
